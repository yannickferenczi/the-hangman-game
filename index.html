Check the rules here
  • The goal of the hangman game is to guess the secret word before the stick figure is hung. Select letters to narrow the word.
  • The gameplay continues until you guess the word or you run out of guesses and the stick figure is hung.
  • Levels are defined as below:
    • Level 1: words of 5 or 6 letters.
    • Level 2: words of 7 or 8 letters.
    • Level 3: words of 9 or 10 letters.
    • Level 4: words of 11 or 12 letters.
    • Idioms: common idioms used in UK or America.
  • Points:
    • You start with 0 Points.
    • You only increase your score if the riddle is entirely solved.
    • When solved, the riddle gives as many points as the amount of letters to guess.
